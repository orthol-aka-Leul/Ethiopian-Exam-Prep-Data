Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

App Name: EthioExam Pro (Ethiopian Exam Prep)

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use the EthioExam Pro mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Our app does not require you to create an account or provide personal identifiers like your name or home address. However, we do process certain data automatically:

  • Device Identifiers: We use the Android Advertising ID to provide personalized ads and for fraud prevention.
  • Usage Progress: We store your quiz scores and unlocked exam status locally on your device to provide a continuous learning experience.
  • App Performance: We may collect anonymous crash logs to improve app stability.

2. Third-Party Services

To keep this educational tool free, we use third-party advertising and content delivery services. These providers may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used for showing banner and rewarded ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads: Used via AdMob Mediation to show rewarded video ads. Unity Privacy Policy
  • GitHub: Used to host and deliver updated question banks to your device.

3. Data Storage (Offline Use)

All your progress data (scores and unlocked years) is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a central database of your individual scores. If you delete the app, your local progress will be lost unless you have backed up your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for Grade 12 students in Ethiopia (typically aged 17+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

5. Your Choices

You can reset your Advertising ID or disable personalized ads through your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads). You can also clear all app data using the "Reset Progress" menu within the app.
