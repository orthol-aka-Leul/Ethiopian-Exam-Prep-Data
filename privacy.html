Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

App Name: EthioExam Pro

1. Information We Collect

EthioExam Pro does not collect personal identity information (PII). We process device IDs via AdMob/Unity for advertising purposes and store study progress locally on your device.

2. Data Deletion

We respect your right to control your data. Users can delete all locally stored study progress, scores, and unlock history at any time without deleting their account (no account is required to use this app).

To delete your data:

  • Open the app and go to the Home screen.
  • Tap the Menu (three dots) in the top right corner.
  • Select "Reset Progress".
  • Confirm the reset. All progress and history will be permanently erased.

Alternatively, clearing the app's cache and data in the Android System Settings will also permanently delete all locally stored information.

3. Third-Party Data

For data processed by our partners (Google AdMob and Unity Ads), you can reset your Advertising ID via your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).
